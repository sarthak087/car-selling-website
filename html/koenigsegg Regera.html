    • About This Car

    The Koenigsegg Regera is a revolutionary hypercar that combines stunning design and cutting-edge technology. Launched in 2016, it features a hybrid powertrain with a twin-turbocharged V8 engine and three electric motors, generating around 1,500 horsepower, allowing it to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.8 seconds. Its innovative Direct Drive system replaces a traditional multi-gear transmission for seamless power delivery, while the sleek carbon fiber body enhances aerodynamics and performance. Inside, the Regera boasts a luxurious cabin equipped with advanced technology, solidifying its status as a masterpiece in the hypercar realm.
