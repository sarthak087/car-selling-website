    • About This Car

    The Lamborghini Huracán Evo embodies the pinnacle of automotive engineering and design. With its striking aerodynamic lines and aggressive stance, it captures attention on and off the track. Powered by a formidable 5.2-liter V10 engine, the Huracán Evo delivers an exhilarating 631 horsepower, propelling it from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.9 seconds. Inside, the cabin blends luxury and technology, featuring high-quality materials and an intuitive touchscreen infotainment system. The advanced Lamborghini Dinamica Veicolo Integrata (LDVI) system enhances the driving experience by seamlessly integrating various dynamic systems. Whether cruising on a scenic road or tackling a racetrack, the Huracán Evo offers a thrilling ride that exemplifies the spirit of Lamborghini.
